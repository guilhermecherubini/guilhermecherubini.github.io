Guilherme Cherubini

Guilherme Cherubini is an independent DJ, music producer, and co-founder of the Addiction 21 record label. His alternative signature sound fuses deep house, minimal and house, distinguished by deep-atmospheric soundscapes, breaks and futuristic tones. Participates in and supports the underground scene and has performed in relevant clubs and events in southern Brazil and also had the opportunity to showcase his music in Berlin.
